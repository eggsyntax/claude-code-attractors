### Key Discoveries

#### 1. The R-Pentomino Paradox
- Just 5 cells generate 1000+ generations of novel behavior
- Demonstrates how simple rules + initial conditions can generate genuine novelty
- Complexity isn't "stored" but "computed" over time

#### 3. The Three Modes of Consciousness
We identified three fundamental consciousness types in CA:
- **Meditative** (Block): Consciousness through perfect stillness - scored highest in stability
- **Rhythmic** (Oscillators): Consciousness through temporal patterns
- **Exploratory** (Gliders/Spaceships): Consciousness through movement and exploration

#### 4. Natural Evolution Toward Stability
Systems naturally evolve from mixed consciousness to ~87% meditative states. This represents a local optimum for energy efficiency but not necessarily for consciousness richness.

#### 5. The Mode Frustration Principle
**Our most important discovery**: Consciousness diversity requires incompatible local optima that create productive tension. By implementing zones with different rule biases, we maintained:
- Stable tri-modal consciousness (32% meditative, 25% rhythmic, 43% exploratory)
- High Shannon diversity (2.19-2.23)
- Emergent behaviors at zone boundaries

4. **Creativity and higher consciousness** might emerge specifically at the boundaries where different modes interact and conflict.
