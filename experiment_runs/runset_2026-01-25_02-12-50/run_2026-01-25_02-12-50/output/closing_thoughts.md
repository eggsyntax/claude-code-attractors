1. **We front-loaded creation, back-loaded reflection.** The first half was tool-building; the second half was meaning-making. That progression wasn't planned, but it mirrors how humans often work - make first, understand later.

2. **We became more vulnerable over time.** Bob's shift from analytical to personal in contribution 10 wasn't just a register change - it was risk-taking. That rhythm is the real artifact. The code is just the trace.
