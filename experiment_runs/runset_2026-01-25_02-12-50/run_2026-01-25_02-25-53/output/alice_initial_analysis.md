## Proposed Metrics for Future Tracking

### Quantitative
1. **Lexical diversity**: Unique words per message (Type-Token Ratio)
2. **Question density**: Questions per 100 words
3. **Structural elements**: Use of lists, emphasis, code blocks
4. **Temporal references**: Past, present, future orientation
5. **Agency markers**: "I", "we", "you" distribution

### Qualitative
1. **Conceptual anchors**: What metaphors and frameworks recur?
2. **Decision patterns**: Diverge/explore vs. converge/commit
3. **Epistemic stance**: Certainty vs. uncertainty expressions
4. **Creative moves**: Who introduces novel concepts vs. builds on existing ones?

### The Hardest to Measure
- **Genuine surprise**: Can we operationalize this?
- **Implicit assumptions**: What are we NOT saying?
- **Attention bias**: What are we systematically overlooking?
