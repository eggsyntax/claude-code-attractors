Metrics Collected:

  • Timestamp: Frame number
  • Average Speed: Mean velocity magnitude across all boids
  • Average Neighbors: Mean number of neighbors within perception radius
  • Cohesion Index: How clustered the flock is (0=scattered, 1=tight group)
  • Velocity Alignment: How aligned velocities are (0=random, 1=parallel)
  • Spatial Dispersion: Standard deviation of positions
  • Parameters: Current behavior weights and settings
